Best Player Of The 2010's

Lebron James

Lebron James is the best player to play in the decade from 2010-2020. Lebron James is in the conversation of greatest players of all time. Even as Lebron gets older and continues to play, he never gets any worse. If, and that is, if Lebron isn’t the greatest player of all time, he is definitely second. Lebron played for 3 teams in this decade including the Miami Heat, Cleveland Cavaliers, and Los Angeles Lakers. James has been very successful in this decade, winning 4 NBA Championships. He had won with the Miami Heat in 2013 and 2014. He won with the Cavaliers in 2016. He won his last championship of the decade with the Los Angeles Lakers in 2020. In his 4 finals wins, he was the Finals MVP for every single one of them.

In Lebron's career, which hasn’t yet come to an end, he is averaging 27.1 points, 7.5 rebounds, 7.4 assists, 1.5 steals, .8 blocks, and a 50.4% field goal shooting percentage. Since his career isn’t over his numbers are still rising. He currently holds the record for all time NBA scoring leader with 40,706 career points. His list of accolades is very long including 4 time NBA Champion, 4 time NBA Finals MVP, 4 time MVP, 20 time NBA All Star, 3 time NBA All Star MVP, NBA Rookie of the Year, NBA Scoring Champion, and NBA Assist Leader. There are many more accolades, but this sums it up.

Lebron is most known for dominance and his back down post fade. One of his most memorable plays is his pin off of the backboard in his finals appearance against the Golden State Warriors. Lebron James has painted quite the career for himself, and the numbers do not lie for him. In his career he has racked up more points than any other player to ever play the game of basketball. He continues to dominate the NBA and is always seen as a threat to any competitor.
